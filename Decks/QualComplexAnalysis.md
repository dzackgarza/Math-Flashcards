Dirichlet's Test
%
If $\theset{a_n}, \theset{b_n}$ satisfy

- $a_{n+1} \leq a_n$
- $a_n \to 0$
- For every $N$, there exists an $M$ such that $\abs{\sum^N b_n} \leq M$

Then $\sum a_n b_n < \infty$.
%
theorem
