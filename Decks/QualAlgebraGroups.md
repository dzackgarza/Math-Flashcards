Sylow Theorems
%
Write $\abs{G} = p^n m$ with $(p, m) = 1$.

# Sylow 1

Writing $\abs{G} = \prod p_i^{n_i}$, there exist Sylow $p_i\dash$subgroups $P_i$ of order $p_i^{n_i}$ for every $i$.

## Sylow 2

All Sylows are conjugate.

# Sylow 3

1. $n_p \equiv 1 \mod p$
2. $n_p = [G: N_G(P)]$
